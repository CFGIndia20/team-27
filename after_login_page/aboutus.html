Our Aim

The Nudge Foundation has recently launched the Future Perfect initiative which aims to focus on the skills most acutely needed to be employable in the service industry for college-growing youth who are just entering the workforce.

It then aims to provide students with online lessons on English language problem solving skills, interpersonal skills and career planning, and thus have employed teachers for these dasses, so that they can be better prepared for upcoming jobs.

Students will have the liberty of choosing any slot they like from the 8 slots available to them in a day. Students in these classes are fixed on a first come first serve basis.

The students will then apply for jobs and will continue receiving lifelong alumni support.
